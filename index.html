Notice

Important Notice Regarding Fees

    • For those joining in beginners and go to next level in intermediate batches, difference Amount of next level will be charged on pro-rata basis for the remaining months.
    • All classes will run on Monday to Saturday except on holidays.
    • Those who register in mid of the month will have to automatically register for next full month.
    • Shreyas school and karyakar's children will be given 50% discount for three months.
    • Each month will be counted from the 1st day of the month.
    • Swimming pool remains closed from December to February.
    • Any member opting for a three-month plan should complete the three months within a 12 month period from the date of enrollment.
    • More than one member can enroll and share card if enrolled for 3 months.
    • Students enrolled in the college can participate in any activity by presenting their college ID card and normal fee structure will be applicable.
    • Only upi and cheque payment will be accepted.
    • Fees Once Paid Will not be refunded.
